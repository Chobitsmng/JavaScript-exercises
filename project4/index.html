Welcome to Foo-G's Pizza!

Nestled in the cozy country village of Elsie, Michigan right on Main St is Foo-g's Pizza. Family and localy owned and opporated, Foo-g's will give you that small town feel with food that can't help but put a smile on your face. With a huge variety of goodies to choose from served by a friendly and inviting staff.
