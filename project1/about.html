About Foo-G's!

Options, thats what you get at Foo-G's Pizza. Our menu has over 25 different subs, various pitas, salads, baked potatoes and bread sticks. Plus a full breakfast menu. (Breakfast served all day) We also have boneless wings, lasagna and nacho's. Come in during the summer for a full selection of ice cream as well. Incase none of those sound good to you, we also make the best pizza in town.

About Elsie, Michigan!

Elsie is a village in Clinton County in the U.S. state of Michigan. The population was 966 at the 2010 census. The village consists of two noncontinuous portions within Duplain Township in the northeast portion of Clinton County. It was incorporated in 1885.
